    Maison Lumière · MMXXVI

    A cabinet
    of light.

    Six rooms. Six objects.
    Each lit by its own private dawn.

    — scroll —

    Chapter I

    L'Aurore

    A single solitaire in rose gold, caught at the precise minute the horizon turns from violet to honey. We lit it with one window and waited three days for the sky.

    Stone
    1.84 ct · brilliant
    Metal
    18k rose gold
    Edition
    Unique

    Chapter II

    Onyx

    Three shards of black tourmaline grown over a decade in the dark, then set on a single beam of cold blue. The room is silent except for the hum of a winter night.

    Material
    Schorl tourmaline
    Base
    Brushed gunmetal
    Edition
    Series of 3

    Chapter III

    Méridien

    A pocket watch suspended in brass light. Forty-two jewels, one tourbillon, one note of a piano held under the dial. It is always a quarter past five.

    Calibre
    ML-42 manuel
    Case
    Yellow gold, 41 mm
    Reserve
    72 hours

    Chapter IV

    Constellation

    Twenty-one diamonds along a circle of platinum, lit by the small ghosts of stars that would have been visible above Paris on the night the maison opened.

    Stones
    21 · 0.12 ct each
    Chain
    Hand-knotted platinum
    Edition
    Series of 12

    Chapter V

    Héritage

    A decanter cut from a single block of lead crystal, lit only by one candle that we replace every morning. The room smells of beeswax and the cellar below it.

    Material
    Crystal · 32% PbO
    Stopper
    Hand-ground
    Edition
    Unique, signed

    Fin

    Crafted in light.

    Each piece exists once.
    Each room, once a year.
